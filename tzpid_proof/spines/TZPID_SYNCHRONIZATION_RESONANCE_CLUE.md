# Synchronization & Resonance-Locking — the mechanism that selects the ratios

The other pillars *observe* rational ratios and scale-invariance (the comma 3/2, the fifth, the avalanche 3/2, the BAO acoustic scale). This pillar supplies the **mechanism that produces them**: a wave bounded by an enclosure becomes a discrete set of oscillators, and coupled oscillators **phase-lock onto rational frequency ratios**. Kuramoto, orbital resonance, and tidal locking are the same physics; "Well, Wall, Wave" is the enclosure that makes it discrete in the first place.

## The chain
1. **Well / Wall / Wave → quantized modes (ID0201).** A wave trapped by a wall/well has a discrete spectrum `f_n = n·v_A/(2πR)`. The enclosure *boundary* turns a continuum into countable oscillators. (Cavity kin: ID3288 modal frequencies, ID3290 Helmholtz boundary pressure, ID0506 Purcell cavity modes.)
2. **Kuramoto coupling (ID0115).** Those oscillators interact: `θ̇_i = ω_i + (K/N) Σ_j sin(θ_j − θ_i)`. Above a critical coupling they synchronize.
3. **Order parameter (ID2222).** Lock is measured by `R(t) = |(1/N) Σ_j e^{iθ_j}|`: 0 = incoherent, 1 = fully locked.
4. **Orbital resonance *is* Kuramoto phase-locking (ID7437).** Your registry already states it: the resonant-angle coupling `sin(k₁φ₁ − k₂φ₂)` is exactly a Kuramoto term. Mean-motion resonances are phase-locks of celestial oscillators.
5. **Tidal torque drives the lock (ID1171).** `τ_tidal(ω, Ω)` is the dissipation that pulls spin toward orbit — the Moon's 1:1, Mercury's 3:2.
6. **Rational-ratio selection (ID0143).** The locked states are rational: `Ω_spin/Ω_orbit = p/q` — 1:1 (tidal lock), 3:2 (Mercury), the Laplace 1:2:4 (Io–Europa–Ganymede).
7. **The π-ladder of allowed ratios (ID7904).** `ω_spin/ω_orbit` is selected from a discrete ladder — the same rational lattice the circle of fifths walks.

## How it fits "the spinal tap"
- **It explains the ratios the other pillars assume.** The comma is built from 3/2; the avalanche/cascade flip is 3/2 ⟺ 2/3; orbital systems lock at p:q. *Phase-locking on the bounded enclosure is why rational ratios appear at all.* This pillar is the generator; the comma and criticality pillars are its consequences.
- **It closes the reciprocal-flip thread.** A resonance `p:q` and its conjugate `q:p` are the same `r ↦ 1/r` inversion as the fifth/descending-fifth and the comma `γ ↔ 1/γ`. Resonance locking is the *dynamical* face of the flip.
- **Well/Wall/Wave = the enclosure, dynamically.** The boundary that quantizes (ID0201, ID3290) is the same nested S³ enclosure whose Hopf projection gives the comma. The enclosure both *holds the modes* (acoustics, Insight 1) and *locks them* (this pillar).
