The Fibonacci sequence obeys F\_n = F\_\{n-1\} + F\_\{n-2\} with F\_0 = 0 and F\_1 = 1. The ratio of successive terms converges to the golden ratio $\phi$ = (1 + sqrt5)/2. A closed-form expression is F\_n = ($\phi$\^{}n -- $\psi$\^{}n)/sqrt5 where $\psi$ = (1 -- sqrt5)/2.
