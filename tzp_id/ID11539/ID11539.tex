\section*{ID11539 --- Paper XI Acoustic Horizon Integral}

\paragraph{Statement.}
The comoving sound horizon is the redshift integral of sound speed over Hubble rate from z_star to infinity.

\paragraph{Canonical equation.}
\[
r_s(z_\star)=\int_{z_\star}^{\infty}\frac{c_s(z)}{H(z)}\,dz
\]
