\section*{ID11465 --- Sn Embedding Sphere Definition}

\paragraph{Statement.}
An n-sphere of radius R is the locus of points in R^{n+1} with squared norm R squared.

\paragraph{Canonical equation.}
\[
S^n(R)=\{x\in\mathbb{R}^{n+1}:x_1^2+\cdots+x_{n+1}^2=R^2\}
\]
